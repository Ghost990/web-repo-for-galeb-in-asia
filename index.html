Our Mission

Background

The Asian market is growing rapidly. China is expected to become the world’s largest economy by 2020 and India is expected to rank fourth on the global economic scale by 2025. Southeast Asia has enjoyed remarkable economic progress in recent years; millions have been lifted out of poverty. This growth offers numerous business opportunities for organizations wanting to gain a foothold in Asia.

Unsurprisingly, Asia is considered a key strategic growth market in various fields. This is not just because of the region's vast population – more than half the current world population lives in Asia – but also because the area continues to develop, innovate profusely and gain more influence on a global scale.

Galeb in Asia

Although Eastern and Southeastern European businesses produces competitive quality products, they are still under represented in Asian market. Our mission is to connect Eastern and Southeastern European small businesses to Asian market and distribute their products in the emerging market.

Small business cannot spend money to buy market share or set up a costly venture in Asian shore. They cannot enter a new market, unless they have a rational expectation they will make profit from the very first sell.
But what they offer is the best in their category, often long lived traditional products. Understanding of these facts is essential for us.

By utilizing the empty containers flowing back From the aspect of Asian people, we help them to access to a unique selections of products for reasonable price.
